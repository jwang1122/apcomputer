## Display Equation

Single "\$" sign makes the equation embeded in the text. $A=\frac 1 2 D \pi ^ 2$, where D is diameter of a circle, A is area of the circle;
And "$$" sign makes the equation separate from the text.

$$
\lim_{x\to\infty} f(x) \tag{1}
$$
